Educational prototype statement for the Energy Companion website.

  • The platform is for informational and educational purposes only and does not guarantee specific savings.

Privacy Policy

Privacy-first summary with minimal data collection.

  • Energy Companion follows a privacy-first approach and collects only the minimum data needed to generate useful insights.
  • The system may collect household energy usage, water usage, and daily or weekly usage patterns.
  • The system does not collect personal identifiers such as name, address, or phone number.
  • Data may be stored locally in the user's browser and is not shared with third parties.
  • Users can clear stored data, stop using the system at any time, and disable optional tracking features.
  • As this is a prototype, basic security measures are used, but production-level security is not guaranteed.
